Department of Wildlife Conservation

The Wildlife Department was established in 1949 to manage the increasing number of Wildlife reserves and protect wildlife. From 1960 onwards, the Department of Wildlife Conservation faced challenges from large-scale development projects, such as the Mahaweli Environmental Project. Since 1993, a special project called "Development of Wildlife Conservation and Protected Area Management" has been launched to ensure the protection of wildlife resources.
